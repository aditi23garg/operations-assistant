# Order Shipping Costs and Pending Orders

## Overview of Shipping Costs Based on Order Value

#### For orders under $200:
Orders valued below $200 incur a **flat $12** shipping fee [Source: shipping_policy.txt].

#### Free Shipping for Larger Orders:
- Orders over $200 qualify for free standard shipping.
  - Standard shipping generally takes 5-7 business days, while express and same-day shipping options are also available under certain conditions.
