Nexus Supply Co. - Shipping Policy

Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
Express shipping: 2-3 business days (additional $25 fee)
Same-day shipping: available in Chicago metro area only ($50 fee)

Orders over $200 qualify for free standard shipping.
Orders under $200 have a flat $12 shipping fee.

Large items (shelving units, desks over 40kg) ship via freight carrier.
Freight orders require a delivery appointment and a loading dock or 
ground-level access at the delivery address.

International shipping is not available at this time.
Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
