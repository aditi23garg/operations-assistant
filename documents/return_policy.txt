Nexus Supply Co. - Return Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
2. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition.
3. Warehouse equipment over $500 requires a return authorization (RA) number 
   before shipping back. Contact support to obtain an RA number.
4. Consumables (paper, cartridges) are non-returnable once opened.
5. Damaged items must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photos.
6. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the item is received.
7. Return shipping costs are covered by Nexus Supply Co. if the item is 
   defective or incorrectly sent. Otherwise the customer pays return shipping.
