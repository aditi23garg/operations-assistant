Nexus Supply Co. - Vendor and Supplier Policy

Approved vendors must be re-evaluated annually.
New vendors require a 3-month probationary period before full approval.

Vendor requirements:
1. Must provide proof of insurance and business registration.
2. Must meet minimum quality standards (ISO 9001 or equivalent).
3. Delivery punctuality must be above 90% over any 3-month period.
4. Price changes require 60 days written notice.

Payment to vendors follows Net 45 terms unless otherwise negotiated.
All vendor contracts must be reviewed and signed by the Operations Manager.
Vendors failing quality or punctuality standards receive a formal warning 
before contract termination.
