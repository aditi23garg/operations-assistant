Nexus Supply Co. - Payment Terms

Standard accounts: Net 30 (payment due 30 days from invoice date)
New accounts (first 3 orders): payment in advance required
Premium accounts (spend over $50,000/year): Net 45 available

Accepted payment methods:
- Bank transfer (preferred)
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) — 1.5% surcharge applies
- Cheque (adds 3 business days to processing)

Late payment policy:
- Reminder sent at Day 35
- 2% monthly interest charged from Day 45
- Account put on hold at Day 60 (no new orders processed)
- Referred to collections at Day 90
