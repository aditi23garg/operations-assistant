Nexus Supply Co. - Warehouse Operations Guidelines

Safety Rules:
1. All staff must wear safety helmets and steel toe boots in the warehouse.
2. Maximum shelf load is 500kg per tier on heavy duty shelving units.
3. Pallet jacks must not be operated at speeds exceeding walking pace.
4. Aisles must remain clear of obstructions at all times.
5. Fire exits must never be blocked.

Stock Management:
- Low stock alert triggers at 20 units for consumables.
- Low stock alert triggers at 5 units for equipment.
- Stock counts are performed every Friday before closing.
- Damaged stock must be tagged and moved to the returns bay immediately.

Receiving Deliveries:
- All deliveries must be checked against the purchase order.
- Discrepancies must be reported to the operations manager within 2 hours.
