Nexus Supply Co. - Company Overview

Nexus Supply Co. is a mid-sized supplier of office and warehouse equipment 
founded in 2015. We serve over 500 business clients across the country.

Our core product lines are:
- Office furniture (desks, chairs, filing cabinets)
- Warehouse equipment (shelving units, pallet jacks, safety gear)
- Office consumables (paper, printer cartridges, stationery)

Head office is located in Chicago, IL.
